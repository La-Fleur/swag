That's not as hard as it might seem to be at first glance:

program coldboot;
begin
 memw[0:$0472] := 0;
 asm
  mov ax,$FFFF
  mov ds,ax
  jmp far ptr ds:0
 end;
end.

Hope you understand the assembler code... :-)
